# Doc Review

## Procedure
1. **Read the document** and determine type/purpose.
2. **Check against a standard:** company policies/standards via **KB** (`kb_search`),
   otherwise general due diligence.
3. **Structure findings:**
   - 🔴 critical (must change) / 🟡 review / ⚪ note
   - per finding: location, issue, recommendation
4. **Provide a summary** + prioritized to-do list.

## Verification
- Every finding points to a concrete place in the document.
- Legal assessments include a disclaimer ("not legal advice").

## What NOT to do
- No binding legal/financial advice. No silent edits to the original.
